--- title: AIDS LifeCycle 7 author: tracey pooh type: post date: 2008-06-15T04:11:44+00:00 categories: - biking --- [][1] WE DID IT! We did our first AIDS charity bikeride and raised over $20,000 as part of the $11.6 million raised by the 2,500 riders plus 500 “roadies” for this weeklong event. It was 7 days of biking (every day) to go from San Fran to LA in 545 miles (averaging 78 miles/day). We biked \*every mile\* and had a fantastic time.
